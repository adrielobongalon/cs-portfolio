Texting Tool


Hey guys! Just want to remind you to treat this like an actual phone. Don't be disrespectful to people with it. And please don't bother jelly, especially if you've never met her. Be responsible.

This texting tool is made using Twilio. Thanks to the guys who made it!

Instructions

Put a phone number in the first box in the form "123-245-7890" (do not forget the hyphens). Then enter a message in the second box.

Click text number to send the message to the number in the box. Click text audrey to send the message to audrey (the person who made this). Clicking text jelly will send the message to jelly, audrey's friend, but please don't bother her. The call buttons do nearly the same thing, except it calls the number and a robotic voice says the message. Please don't call me. or jelly.
