Instructions

Originally, this was supposed to be a "box game" where you control a small box. I sized down the box to a dot, and modified it so that the box leaves a trail behind. Now it acts like an Etch a Sketch!


You can use the arrow keys to move the dot.

For those who prefer a more classic approach, the z, x, ,, and . keys. It simulates a clockwise and anticlockwise turn of each knob.

Dvorak user? Don't worry; I got you covered. You can use the qwerty equivalents ;, q, w, and v.

The buttons should be self-explanatory.
